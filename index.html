Online Gospel Resources

If you have a smartphone, the apps are the best way to get content like:
  • LDS Tools - Ward directory, map, org chart, ward calendar, and ability to create custom lists to which you can send group messages
  • Gospel Library - The scriptures, "Come, Follow Me" manuals, general conference, hymns, and more. You can read, watch video, or listen while you do other things.
  • LDS Music - Hymns, Children's Songbook, and more. Words, written music, and audio.
See the menu links at the top-right of this page to access these resources on the web if you don't yet have the apps. You can download the apps for free in your devices app store (Google Play or App Store).
